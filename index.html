How We Work

The WAG Process

A proven three-step process built around your life — not a template.

01

Welcome

Before anything else, we take time to get to know you — your family, financial situation, goals, and any gaps in your current plan. We identify potential improvements across savings, investing, insurance, and estate planning. We never take a cookie-cutter approach.

02

Advise

Once we understand where you stand and where you want to go, we develop a customized, comprehensive financial plan — incorporating all aspects of your financial life into a single, holistic strategy. We walk you through every recommendation before anything is implemented.

03

Guide

Once your plan is implemented, we continuously monitor and update it as your life and the markets evolve. We meet regularly, keep you informed, and remain your trusted advisor through every stage — not just the first one.
